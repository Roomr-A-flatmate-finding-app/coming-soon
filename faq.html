🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used by websites to make them work more efficiently and to provide reporting information.

Cookies can be "session cookies" — which expire when you close your browser — or "persistent cookies" — which remain on your device for a defined period or until you delete them. Cookies set by the website you're visiting are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by other organisations are called "third-party cookies."

We also use similar tracking technologies like web beacons (tiny transparent images), local storage, and session storage. This policy covers all of these technologies collectively.

✅ Quick version: Cookies help us keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how you use Roomrr so we can keep improving it. Some are strictly necessary; others are optional and require your consent.

✅ Consent

When you first visit Roomrr, we display a cookie consent banner. By clicking "Accept All" you consent to all cookie categories. By clicking "Manage Preferences" you can choose which optional categories to enable.

  • Essential cookies are deployed immediately on visit without requiring consent, as they are necessary for the Platform to function.
  • Functional, Analytics, and Marketing cookies are only placed after you provide consent.
  • You may withdraw your consent at any time using the preference panel on this page or by contacting us at privacy@roomrr.app.
  • Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of prior cookie usage.

We record your consent preferences with a timestamp. Your consent record is stored for 12 months, after which we will ask for your consent again.
